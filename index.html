Welcome to my academic homepage. I am a machine learning researcher. I received my Bachlor's degree in Computer Science and Technology at Tianjin University .

My primary research interest is building next-generation vision-language-action (VLA) systems for reliable instruction-specified manipulations in messy real-world scenes typically involving strong occlusions and variable lighting. Some of my research keywords can be found below:

  • Embodied agents: Robotic manipulation, Motion planning, Reinforcement learning and imitation
  • Multimodal learning: Vision and language, Visual reasoning, 3D vision, Generalist models
  • 3D Perception: Human pose estimation, 3D reconstruction, Point cloud understanding, Visual tracking
  • AI for Healthcare: Medical image analysis, Physiological signal processing (EEG/ECG)
